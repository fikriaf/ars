1. **AI Agent DeFi** - Autonomous agents executing DeFi operations

3. **Algorithmic Monetary Policy** - Autonomous stablecoin/liquidity management

This analysis identifies key competitors across these domains and highlights ICB's unique positioning.

## Direct Competitors

### 1. MetaDAO (Solana)

**Website:** https://metadao.fi  
**Category:** Futarchy Governance Protocol

#### What They Do
- Meta-governance protocol enabling DAOs to use futarchy for decision-making
- Prediction markets for policy proposals
- "Vote on values, bet on beliefs" model
- Ownership tokens for governance participation

#### Strengths
- ✅ First-mover in Solana futarchy space
- ✅ Proven prediction market infrastructure
- ✅ Strong community and documentation
- ✅ Established partnerships with other DAOs
- ✅ Ownership token model aligns incentives

#### Weaknesses
- ❌ Generic governance tool (not DeFi-specific)
- ❌ No autonomous agent integration
- ❌ No monetary policy focus
- ❌ Requires human participation
- ❌ Not agent-first architecture

#### ICB Differentiation
| Feature | MetaDAO | ICB |
|---------|---------|-----|
| **Futarchy** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| **Agent-First** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| **Monetary Policy** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| **DeFi Integration** | ❌ Generic | ✅ Deep (8 protocols) |
| **Autonomous Execution** | ❌ Human-driven | ✅ Agent-driven |
| **ILI/ICR Metrics** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

**Verdict:** MetaDAO is a governance infrastructure provider. ICB uses futarchy for a specific purpose (monetary policy) with agent-first execution.
